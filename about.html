About FurCreates

We Are India’s No.1 Stray Dog Rescue – 15+ Years of Saving Lives

At FurCreates, we are proud to be India’s leading nonprofit organization for stray dog rescue and adoption, with over 15 years of dedicated service. Our journey began with a single mission: to give every stray dog a chance at a safe, happy life.

Since our founding, we have rescued and rehabilitated thousands of dogs in need—injured, abandoned, hungry, or mistreated. Today, FurCreates stands as a symbol of compassion, trust, and hope for countless voiceless souls across the country.

Our Mission

We believe that every life matters. Whether it’s a pup wandering the streets or an injured dog left to suffer, we step in to provide urgent care, shelter, and love. Our team of rescuers, vets, and volunteers work around the clock to ensure that no dog is left behind.
